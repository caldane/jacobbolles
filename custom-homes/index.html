Custom Homes

Don't Settle for a Cookie-Cutter Home

Experience the elegance of a tailor-made home designed exclusively for your lifestyle and vision.

Designing and building your own home gives you the freedom to create something truly exceptional—an expression of your taste, needs, and lifestyle. At Jacob Bolles Construction, our skilled contractors bring over seven years of experience to every custom build in Oklahoma City and surrounding areas.

From conceptual design to the final touches, our team will guide you through each step—drafting blueprints, sourcing premium materials, and crafting a home that’s as beautiful as it is functional. Not sure where to start? Contact us today to begin the journey toward your dream home.

Let Your Imagination Run Wild

Our dedicated team is equipped to take on complex designs and personalized touches that make your home uniquely yours. We specialize in creating custom homes with standout features such as:

  • Skylights for natural elegance
  • Built-in shelves for form and function
  • Cozy reading nooks
  • Open or closed floor plan configurations
  • High-end roofing options: metal, asphalt, or slate

Why Build a Custom Home?

Choosing a custom home means investing in a space that’s designed around your life—not someone else’s. When you partner with Jacob Bolles Construction, you'll enjoy benefits like:

  • A home tailored perfectly to your needs and style
  • The freedom to include all the features you’ve always wanted
  • Optimized space that reflects your lifestyle
  • A smooth process that bypasses the stress of the traditional housing market

We’re proud to offer custom home construction services in Oklahoma City and the surrounding communities. Reach out today to get started.
