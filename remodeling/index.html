Remodeling

Transform the Space You’re In

Professional residential and commercial remodeling services in Oklahoma City and surrounding areas.

If you’ve outgrown your current layout or want to refresh the appearance of your home or business, a full remodel may be the ideal solution. At Jacob Bolles Construction, we provide expert remodeling services tailored to your goals, style, and budget—without the disruption of moving.

Whether you're re-imagining your living space or upgrading your commercial property, our team delivers quality craftsmanship and exceptional attention to detail at every stage of the renovation process.

Re-imagine Your Space With Purpose

Our remodeling services are designed to enhance both form and function. Common upgrades include:

  • Modernizing kitchens by opening walls and improving flow
  • Upgrading flooring with hardwood, tile, or other premium materials
  • Installing custom lighting solutions for enhanced ambiance and efficiency
  • Revitalizing exterior finishes for improved curb appeal

From subtle updates to full-scale renovations, we work closely with you to ensure the finished result reflects your vision and exceeds expectations.

A Proven Process, Exceptional Results

We approach every remodeling project with a clear, streamlined process to ensure timely delivery and outstanding outcomes. Here's what you can expect when working with us:

  • Initial consultation and free, detailed estimate
  • Custom blueprints and project planning
  • Procurement of high-quality materials and precise measurements
  • Professional installation of all elements—from flooring to fixtures
  • Final walkthrough to confirm satisfaction and craftsmanship quality

Our experienced team is committed to delivering spaces that are both beautiful and functional. Contact us today to learn more about our remodeling services in Oklahoma City and nearby areas.
